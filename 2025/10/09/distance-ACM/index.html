闵可夫斯基距离(Minkowski distance)

nnn维空间中两点X(x1,x2,…,xn)X(x_1, x_2, \dots, x_n)X(x1​,x2​,…,xn​)与Y(y1,y2,…,yn)Y(y_1, y_2, \dots, y_n)Y(y1​,y2​,…,yn​)之间的闵可夫斯基距离:

D(X,Y)=(∑i=1n∣xi−yi∣p)1p.D(X, Y) = \left(\sum_{i=1}^n \left\vert x_i - y_i \right\vert ^p\right)^{\frac{1}{p}}. D(X,Y)=(i=1∑n​∣xi​−yi​∣p)p1​.

欧式距离(p=2p = 2p=2)

nnn维空间中欧式距离:

∥AB→∥=(x11−x21)2+(x12−x22)2+⋅⋅⋅+(x1n−x2n)2=∑i=1n(x1i−x2i)2\begin{aligned} \lVert\overrightarrow{AB}\rVert &= \sqrt{\left ( x_{11} - x_{21} \right )^2 + \left ( x_{12} - x_{22} \right )^2 + \cdot \cdot \cdot +\left ( x_{1n} - x_{2n} \right )^2}\\ &= \sqrt{\sum_{i = 1}^{n}(x_{1i} - x_{2i})^2} \end{aligned} ∥AB∥​=(x11​−x21​)2+(x12​−x22​)2+⋅⋅⋅+(x1n​−x2n​)2​=i=1∑n​(x1i​−x2i​)2​​

两个整点计算欧氏距离时，答案往往是浮点型，因此导致精度误差！

曼哈顿距离(p=1p = 1p=1)

nnn维空间中曼哈顿距离:

d(A,B)=∣x1−y1∣+∣x2−y2∣+⋅⋅⋅+∣xn−yn∣=∑i=1n∣xi−yi∣\begin{aligned} d(A,B) &= |x_1 - y_1| + |x_2 - y_2| + \cdot \cdot \cdot + |x_n - y_n|\\ &= \sum_{i = 1}^{n}|x_i - y_i| \end{aligned} d(A,B)​=∣x1​−y1​∣+∣x2​−y2​∣+⋅⋅⋅+∣xn​−yn​∣=i=1∑n​∣xi​−yi​∣​

三角不等式:

d(i,j)≤d(i,k)+d(k,j)d(i,j)\leq d(i,k)+d(k,j) d(i,j)≤d(i,k)+d(k,j)

切比雪夫距离(p→∞p \to \inftyp→∞)

nnn维空间中切比雪夫距离:

d(x,y)=max⁡{∣x1−y1∣,∣x2−y2∣,⋅⋅⋅,∣xn−yn∣}=max⁡{∣xi−yi∣}(i∈[1,n])\begin{aligned} d(x,y) &= \max\begin{Bmatrix} |x_1 - y_1|,|x_2 - y_2|,\cdot \cdot \cdot,|x_n - y_n|\end{Bmatrix} \\ &= \max\begin{Bmatrix} |x_i - y_i|\end{Bmatrix}(i \in [1, n])\end{aligned} d(x,y)​=max{∣x1​−y1​∣,∣x2​−y2​∣,⋅⋅⋅,∣xn​−yn​∣​}=max{∣xi​−yi​∣​}(i∈[1,n])​

曼哈顿距离与切比雪夫距离

曼哈顿距离

d(A,B)=∣x1−x2∣+∣y1−y2∣=max⁡{x1−x2+y1−y2,x1−x2+y2−y1,x2−x1+y1−y2,x2−x1+y2−y1}=max⁡(∣(x1+y1)−(x2+y2)∣,∣(x1−y1)−(x2−y2)∣)\begin{aligned} d(A,B)&=|x_1 - x_2| + |y_1 - y_2|\\ &=\max\begin{Bmatrix} x_1 - x_2 + y_1 - y_2, x_1 - x_2 + y_2 - y_1,x_2 - x_1 + y_1 - y_2, x_2 - x_1 + y_2 - y_1\end{Bmatrix}\\ &= \max(|(x_1 + y_1) - (x_2 + y_2)|, |(x_1 - y_1) - (x_2 - y_2)|) \end{aligned} d(A,B)​=∣x1​−x2​∣+∣y1​−y2​∣=max{x1​−x2​+y1​−y2​,x1​−x2​+y2​−y1​,x2​−x1​+y1​−y2​,x2​−x1​+y2​−y1​​}=max(∣(x1​+y1​)−(x2​+y2​)∣,∣(x1​−y1​)−(x2​−y2​)∣)​

A(x1,y1)与B(x2,y2)A(x_1, y_1)与B(x_2, y_2)A(x1​,y1​)与B(x2​,y2​)的曼哈顿距离就是(x1+y1,x1−y1)与(x2+y2,x2−y2)(x_1 + y1, x_1 - y_1)与(x_2 + y_2, x_2 - y_2)(x1​+y1,x1​−y1​)与(x2​+y2​,x2​−y2​)的切比雪夫距离

切比雪夫距离

d(A,B)=max⁡{∣x1−x2∣,∣y1−y2∣}=∣x1+y12−x2+y22∣+∣x1−y12−x2−y22∣\begin{aligned} d(A,B)&=\max\begin{Bmatrix} |x_1 - x_2|,|y_1 - y_2|\end{Bmatrix}\\ &=\left|\dfrac{x_1 + y_1}{2}-\dfrac{x_2 + y_2}{2}\right|+\left|\dfrac{x_1 - y_1}{2}-\dfrac{x_2 - y_2}{2}\right| \end{aligned} d(A,B)​=max{∣x1​−x2​∣,∣y1​−y2​∣​}=∣∣∣∣∣​2x1​+y1​​−2x2​+y2​​∣∣∣∣∣​+∣∣∣∣∣​2x1​−y1​​−2x2​−y2​​∣∣∣∣∣​​

结论

  1. 将切比雪夫坐标系旋转45∘45^{\circ}45∘后再缩小一半得到曼哈顿坐标系
  2. 将每个点(x,y)(x, y)(x,y)转化为(x+y,x−y)(x + y, x - y)(x+y,x−y)，新坐标下的切比雪夫距离就是原坐标系的曼哈顿距离
  3. 将每个点(x,y)(x, y)(x,y)转化为(x+y2,x−y2)(\frac {x + y} {2}, \frac {x - y} {2})(2x+y​,2x−y​)，新坐标下的曼哈顿距离就是原坐标系的切比雪夫距离
